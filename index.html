What is Belphegor's Prime?

Belphegor's Prime is a palindromic prime number with a special structure: it's a 1, followed by 13 zeros, then 666 (the Number of the Beast), then 13 zeros, and ending with 1.

This number was named by mathematician Clifford A. Pickover after Belphegor, one of the Seven Princes of Hell, who is known for helping people make discoveries.
